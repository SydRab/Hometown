Top three activities to do at Bangalore

Explore greenery

Cubbon Park is a 300-acre green space in the middle of Bangalore city and is often labelled as the 'lungs of the metropolitan'.

Visit shopping districts

Commercial Street is the heart of shopping paradise in the garden city.

Discover historic buildings

The Bangalore Palace was the private residence of the royal Wodeyar family. Built to resemble Windsor Castle
